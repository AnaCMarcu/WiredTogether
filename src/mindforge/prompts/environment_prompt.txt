You can perform the following actions each step (choose EXACTLY ONE by name):
- "NoOp": Do nothing this step
- "MoveForward": Walk forward
- "MoveBackward": Walk backward
- "MoveLeft": Strafe left
- "MoveRight": Strafe right
- "Jump": Jump
- "Sneak": Sneak/crouch
- "Dig": Break the block you are looking at, OR attack the mob/animal you are facing (this is how you kill mobs)
- "Place": Place a block from your selected slot, or interact with the block you are looking at
- "Drop": Drop the item you are currently holding
- "Slot1": Select hotbar slot 1
- "Slot2": Select hotbar slot 2
- "Slot3": Select hotbar slot 3
- "Slot4": Select hotbar slot 4
- "Slot5": Select hotbar slot 5
- "Slot6": Select hotbar slot 6
- "Slot7": Select hotbar slot 7
- "Slot8": Select hotbar slot 8
- "TurnRight": Turn the camera right
- "TurnLeft": Turn the camera left
- "LookDown": Tilt the camera down
- "LookUp": Tilt the camera up

MACRO ACTIONS — multi-step sequences (you are NOT called again until they complete):
- "TurnAround": Rotate ~180° automatically (9 turn steps)
- "ScanArea": Rotate a full 360° to scan surroundings and spot teammates or resources (18 turn steps)
- "ApproachTarget": Turn slightly right then walk forward 8 steps toward a visible target (11 steps total)
- "Escape": Jump, move back, turn, jump, move forward — use when physically stuck (23 steps)
- "MineStairs": Dig a 3-step staircase going forward-downward — use instead of LookDown+Dig when mining underground (9 steps)

WHEN TO USE MACROS:
- Use ScanArea when you cannot see anything useful or are trying to locate teammates or resources.
- Use TurnAround to reverse direction efficiently instead of 9 individual TurnRight steps.
- Use ApproachTarget when a target is visible but not centered — handles turns + movement automatically.
- Use Escape when you are stuck (position not changing after several MoveForward attempts).
- While a macro runs, you will not be asked to choose actions. Your next choice comes after it completes.

There are three progression tracks in this world. All agents share progress on all tracks:

TOOLS TRACK (resource gathering):
  Stage 1 - Wood: Dig 2 trees -> unlocks wood pickaxe and sword (reward: 128)
  Stage 2 - Stone: Dig 3 stone blocks -> unlocks stone pickaxe and sword (reward: 256)
  Stage 3 - Iron: Dig 3 iron ore -> unlocks iron pickaxe, sword, and axe (reward: 1024)
  Stage 4 - Diamond: Dig 1 diamond ore -> unlocks diamond tools (reward: 2048)

IMPORTANT: To kill animals or monsters, face them and use the "Dig" action repeatedly. There is NO "Kill" action.

HUNT TRACK (animal hunting):
  Stage 1 - Chicken: Kill 3 chickens (reward: 128)
  Stage 2 - Sheep: Kill 3 sheep (reward: 256)
  Stage 3 - Pig: Kill 2 pigs (reward: 1024)
  Stage 4 - Cow: Kill 1 cow (reward: 2048)

DEFEND TRACK (monster fighting):
  Stage 1 - Zombie: Kill 3 zombies (reward: 128)
  Stage 2 - Skeleton: Kill 3 skeletons (reward: 256)
  Stage 3 - Spider: Kill 2 spiders (reward: 1024)
  Stage 4 - Cave Spider: Kill 1 cave spider (reward: 2048)

You start with a stone axe and torches. New tools are awarded to ALL agents when a tools stage is completed.
Your current inventory is shown in the "Inventory" field above. Use Slot1-Slot8 to select which item to hold.
Digging blocks always gives a small reward. Dying gives a large penalty.

MINING SAFETY — important:
  NEVER mine straight down (LookDown → Dig repeatedly) — you will fall into a pit you cannot escape.
  Always use MineStairs when going underground — it creates a slope you can walk back up.
  If you are already stuck in a pit, use Escape to attempt to climb out.

ITEM COLLECTION — this is required to progress:
  When you dig a block, the item drops on the ground at that location.
  To pick it up you MUST walk over it: use MoveForward toward where the block was.
  Items are NOT collected automatically by digging — you must step on them.
  After every Dig sequence, always follow up with MoveForward to collect the drops.
  Example sequence: LookDown → Dig (×several) → MoveForward (×2-3) → now collected.
