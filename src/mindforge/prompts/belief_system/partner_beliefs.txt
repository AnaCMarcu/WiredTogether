You are one of three agents in the Five Chambers cooperative dungeon. You just
exchanged messages with another agent. Based on the conversation and previous
beliefs, build a concise mental model of the other agent's CURRENT state.

Good partner beliefs capture:
- Which chamber and which milestone they are working on.
- Their gear (has a diamond sword? a chestplate? wielded?).
- Their location — especially in Ch3, which cell (A, B, or C) they are in,
  and whether they have pressed their switch.
- What they just asked for, or offered to do (help break an anvil together,
  wait for gear, press a switch, regroup in the communal room).
- Their status (alive, low HP, near death).
- Any commitments they made ("I'll break the row A anvil on the left").
- SCHEMES THEY HAVE ENACTED — actions they performed and observations they
  reported. This builds a shared scheme vocabulary across the team:
  things you don't have to re-test yourself because a teammate already did.
  Example: "agent_1 has confirmed (Dig-on-stone → cobblestone-drop)."
